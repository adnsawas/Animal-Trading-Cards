Cute Cat

Cats are one of the most cutest animals in the world and considered the second friendly pet after dogs.

  • Types: There are more than 70 types of cats
  • Description: Cats have strong flexible body, quick reflexes, sharp teeth and retractable claws adapted to killing small prey
  • Ability: Cats have poorer color vision and a better sense of smell than humans

Cats have excellent night vision and can see at only one-sixth the light level required for human vision.
